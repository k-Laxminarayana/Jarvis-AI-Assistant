# Jarvis AI Assistant 🤖

A voice-controlled AI assistant built using Python.

## Features
. Voice Recognition
. Text to Speech
. Open Google
. Open YouTube
. Open ChatGPT
. Open Notepad
. Open Calculator
. Open Paint
. Tell Time
. Tell Date
. Exit using voice command

## Technologies Used
. Python
. SpeechRecognition
. PyAudio
. pyttsx3
. Edge-TTS
. Playsound
